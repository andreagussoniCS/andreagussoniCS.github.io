Week 2-3 discussion

The first discussion we encounter is about information system failures. I read about a lot of interesting cases from the posts of my peers from the stock exchange failure to the airlines system crash. Every failure had its huge impact in the life of so many people that it makes a computing professional think. The system we develop or aspire to are virtual, non tangible but at those scales they do have an incredible impact on the life of so many people that it makes you ponder about the importance of testing and quality of a software and the same approach should be maintained for projects of smaller sizes and maybe less importance.
