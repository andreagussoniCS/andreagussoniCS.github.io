Unit 2

Kill chain

Kill chain is a cyber attack strategy, it involves attacking a service supplier to reach one or more of its clients. The strategy works if the supplier has privileged access to clients' information system and that was the case with the Solar wind case. SolarWind is a major software company based in the US. Leveraging a breach the hackers found in the Orion management system provided by SolarWind the group managed to get access to more than 30.000 organizations and it's the biggest attack of its kind to date. The attackers started injecting malicious code with the Orion software updates from 2019 to 2021 without being noticed. The attack was so sophisticated that it took more than one year to be discovered and the impact, as well as the purpose of the attack are yet to be fully discovered.
